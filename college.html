    Jaya Prakash Narayan College of Engineering (JPNCE), Mahabubnagar was established in 1997. It was established by experienced Academic Visionaries and Industrialists with a view to provide high quality education in the technical field and follow principles, morals, ethics and ideals of Bharat Ratna Lok Nayak Late Sri Jayaprakash Narayan. Hence the college is named after him.
