Kulkacherla:

Kulkacherla is a village & mandal headquarters in Vikarabad district. it consist of total 32 sub villages.Most of the population here is dependent on agriculture.Total population of the village is upto 10500.

Facalities:


Kulkacharla Mandal has 29 grampanchayaths. A famous temple named Pambanda Rameshwaralayam is located 2 km from Kulkacharla.
